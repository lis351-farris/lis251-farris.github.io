Table Tennis or Ping Pong, is a sport that I never really took seriously or even considered a sport, until I joined the table tennis club here at UW! The people that attend all love and appreciate the inner workings of the sport which proved to me that it's more than just a game to play.

Video games are not just a hobby for me, they're a passion a lot of my childhood has been spent playing them, a lot of people have an unfortunate misconception about them, which is that they are just a waste of time. In my opinion, that cannot be further from the truth, as I've learned a lot of history from them, they helped me garner problem solving skills by solving puzzles and they have allowed me to practice my creativity freely!
